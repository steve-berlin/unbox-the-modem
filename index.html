Unbox the Modem

A plain-English course and hands-on tutorial about the Nokia G-010G-P fiber modem: how it works on the inside, and how to explore it safely — starting from zero experience.

Who this is for: complete beginners. No Linux, electronics, networking or programming needed. Every new word is explained before it's used, and there's a full glossary.

Safety first: opening or changing hardware and low-level software carries real risk to the device — and, with mains-powered gear, to you. Always understand a step and make a backup before doing it. Only work on a device you own or have clear permission to touch (see Part 19).
