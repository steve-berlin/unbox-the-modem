Nokia G-010G-P — From Scratch

A plain-English course and hands-on tutorial that teaches how this fiber modem works on the inside, and how to explore it safely — starting from zero experience.

Who this is for: complete beginners. No Linux, electronics, networking or programming needed. Every new word is explained before it's used, and there's a full glossary.

How to use this course

  • Read lessons in order — each builds on the last.
  • Do the quick quiz at the end of every lesson to check yourself.
  • Keep the glossary open in another tab for any unfamiliar word.

Safety first: opening or changing hardware and low-level software carries real risk to the device (and, with mains-powered gear, to you). Always understand a step and make a backup before doing it. This site teaches understanding first, changes second.
